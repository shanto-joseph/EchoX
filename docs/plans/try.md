Pro-Level Feature Suggestions
Auto-Switching by App (Process Detection): Imagine if the user didn't even have to press a shortcut. You could add a feature where if the app detects Spotify.exe opening, it switches to the "Music Profile" (Bluetooth speakers). If it detects csgo.exe or Valorant.exe, it instantly switches to the "Gaming Profile" (Headset + Mic).

Per-Profile Volume Memory: Different headsets have different volume sensitivities. Let users save a master volume level inside the profile. When they switch to their "Late Night Gaming" profile, it not only changes the output device but automatically drops the Windows volume to 15% so they don't blow their eardrums out.

The "Unplugged" Fallback (Device State Monitoring): What happens if a user's wireless headset battery dies or they unplug their USB mic? Your app should detect that the active profile's device just disconnected and automatically fall back to the "Default Profile" (like the laptop speakers) so they don't lose audio entirely.

Dark Mode / Custom UI Themes: Since you emphasized "Better UI/UX," give the user the ability to make that bottom-right OSD (On-Screen Display) popup match their system theme. Let them pick a dark mode, light mode, or a custom accent color for the notifications.

Audio Meter in the Tray:
When the user clicks the system tray icon, show a tiny live green volume bar next to the current mic. It gives them instant visual confirmation that their mic isn't muted and is picking up sound.
